About Us

Revolutionizing Blood Cell Detection

We are transforming the way blood cell analysis is performed by leveraging powerful deep learning and object detection techniques. Our innovative system replaces manual microscopic examination with a faster, automated, and highly accurate solution.
Using the YOLO (You Only Look Once) model, our platform processes high-resolution microscopic images to detect and classify various blood cells such as RBCs and WBCs. The system rapidly identifies and labels cells in real-time, enabling efficient diagnostics and reducing human error—paving the way for smarter hematology.
